Welcome to Tank Battle Ninja!

A 3D multiplayer tank battle game.

How to Play:

Your objective is to get the most points by shooting other tanks. You'll get 100 points for each opponent's tank you shoot. But watch out - you'll loose 50 points each time you are shot. If you loose all your health (aka die), you'll fly back to your base to start over.

Games last three minutes, and the player with the most points at the end wins!

Controls:

  • Forward: up arrow
  • Back: down arrow
  • Left: left arrow
  • right: right arrow
  • Shoot: space bar
  • Overhead view: 'x' key
